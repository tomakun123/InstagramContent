Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This service respects user privacy and only collects data necessary to provide TikTok authentication and video publishing functionality.

Information Collected

When a user connects their TikTok account, the service may collect basic account information provided by TikTok (such as user ID or display name) and access tokens required for authentication and content publishing.

Use of Information

Collected information is used solely to authenticate users and enable user-initiated video uploads and publishing to their own TikTok account.

Data Sharing

This service does not sell or share personal data with third parties. TikTok data is accessed only through official TikTok for Developers APIs.

Data Storage

Authentication data is stored securely and only for as long as required to provide the service.

User Control

Users may revoke access at any time through their TikTok account settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the site owner through the main website.
